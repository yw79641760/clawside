## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is ClawSide free?

Yes, ClawSide is completely free to use.

### Does it work offline?

ClawSide requires a local LLM to be running. It doesn't send data to the cloud.

### Which browsers are supported?

ClawSide works on Chrome and Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Edge, Brave, etc.).

### How do I connect a local LLM?

See our [Configuration Guide](configuration) for step-by-step instructions.
